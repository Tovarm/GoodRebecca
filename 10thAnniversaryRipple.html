10th Anniversary Ripple

  • Start Date:  April 5, 2013

    End Date:  May 30, 2013

    Yarn:  Knit Picks Brava Worsted

    Needle/Hook Size:  5.5mm (I)

    Project Notes:  Anniversary is June 3rd. Not too much time…
    May 30th:     All the ends woven in, and border done! For the border I did one round of double crochet along the two long sides, and on the two short sides I alternated sc, hdc, dc, tc to even out the ripples. Then I did a second round of dc, followed by a round of hdc in the purple. For the edging I used the bobble-shell edging from Lucy of Attic 24, which I love. It came out to around 4' x 5' and is all ready to send out!
